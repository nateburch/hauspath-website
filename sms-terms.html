SMS / Text Messaging Terms

Last updated: March 31, 2026

These SMS Terms describe the text messaging services provided by HausPath, LLC ("HausPath") and apply to all users who opt in to receive text messages from HausPath.

1. Consent & Opt-In

To receive text messages from HausPath, you may opt in through one of the following methods:

  1. On our website: Visit our Contact page, enter your mobile phone number in the phone number field, check the SMS consent checkbox, and submit the form.
  2. In your account: Log in to your HausPath account, navigate to account settings, enter your mobile phone number, and explicitly enable SMS notifications by toggling on text message alerts.

By providing your mobile phone number and completing one of the opt-in methods above, you consent to receive automated text messages from HausPath, LLC. These messages are related to your account activity, appointment reminders, and platform notifications — they are not marketing or promotional messages.

Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing or using HausPath.

SMS Program Disclosure:

  • Program name: HausPath Account Notifications
  • Message sender: HausPath, LLC
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your account activity and notification preferences.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Contact your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.
  • To opt out: Reply STOP to any message from HausPath to unsubscribe. You will receive a single confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.
  • For help: Reply HELP to any message from HausPath, or email support@hauspath.com.
  • Consent is not required as a condition of purchase or use of HausPath.
  • Privacy: Your phone number and messaging data are handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not sell, share, or rent your phone number to third parties.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by replying STOP to any message, disabling SMS notifications in your account settings, or contacting support@hauspath.com.

2. Types of Messages

HausPath may send you the following types of text messages:

  • Activity alerts — Notifications about high-priority activities detected in your workflow
  • Appointment reminders — Reminders for upcoming scheduled events
  • Status updates — Changes to items you are tracking
  • Follow-up reminders — Reminders about pending items that require your attention
  • Account notifications — Security alerts, login verification, and account-related messages

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your account activity, notification preferences, and the number of active items in your workflow. You can customize which types of notifications generate text messages in your account settings.

4. Costs

HausPath does not charge for text messages, but message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Contact your carrier for details about your messaging plan.

5. Opting Out

You may opt out of receiving text messages from HausPath at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message from HausPath
  • Updating your notification preferences in your HausPath account settings
  • Contacting us at support@hauspath.com

After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message. No further text messages will be sent unless you re-opt in.

6. Getting Help

For assistance with text messaging, you may:
